# FreeEed 2027 Vision

## Purpose

FreeEed began as an open-source eDiscovery platform focused on making document processing, search, and review accessible to organizations that could not afford expensive commercial solutions.

By 2027, FreeEed will evolve into a broader AI-powered platform for legal, investigative, disclosure, and data-readiness workflows while remaining grounded in transparency, affordability, and user control.

## Core Principle

AI is not the product.

Data understanding is the product.

Organizations struggle not because they lack AI models, but because they cannot efficiently understand, organize, review, and act on their information.

FreeEed helps users transform large collections of documents into actionable knowledge.

## Strategic Direction

### 1. Legal and eDiscovery

Continue supporting:

* Processing
* Search
* Review
* Analytics
* Production

for law firms, corporations, investigators, and government agencies.

AI should accelerate legal review while keeping humans in control.

### 2. Investigations

Support:

* Internal investigations
* Law enforcement
* Regulatory inquiries
* Inspector General reviews

Future capabilities:

* Actor identification
* Relationship analysis
* Timeline generation
* Case summaries
* Evidence navigation

### 3. Government Disclosure and FOIA

Provide a low-cost alternative for:

* FOIA requests
* Public records requests
* Disclosure workflows

including:

* Collection
* Review
* Redaction
* Publication
* Auditability

Government agencies should not need million-dollar platforms to satisfy transparency requirements.

### 4. AI Data Readiness

Enable organizations to prepare information for AI initiatives.

Capabilities include:

* PII detection
* Sensitive data identification
* Metadata enrichment
* Classification
* Summarization
* Knowledge extraction

FreeEed becomes the governed data layer that prepares information for AI systems.

## The FreeEed Viewer

A portable, offline embodiment of the vision: hand someone a finished production and let them work with it directly — no install, no servers, nothing leaving their machine.

Four capabilities:

* **Search** — find anything in the production, offline.
* **Review** — read and navigate documents.
* **Notes** — capture findings that travel back to the case.
* **AI Case Chat** — ask questions of the case and get grounded, cited answers.

The Viewer carries the product principles into a single tool: **Open** and **Affordable** (free, runs from a thumb drive), **Transparent** and human-in-the-loop (AI surfaces and explains; the user decides), and **Practical** (review where the work happens — courtroom, field, agency). To stay forensically sound, AI Case Chat runs against the bundled production with a local model — no outbound calls.

## Human-in-the-Loop AI

FreeEed does not seek to replace attorneys, investigators, compliance professionals, or reviewers.

Instead, FreeEed uses AI to:

* Reduce manual effort
* Accelerate understanding
* Surface important information
* Improve consistency

Human experts remain responsible for decisions.

## Product Principles

### Open

Open-source remains a core differentiator.

### Transparent

Users should understand how results are generated.

### Affordable

Organizations should not be excluded because of budget.

### Practical

Features should solve real user problems rather than follow industry hype.

## Success Metrics

By 2027:

* Active user community
* Sustainable recurring revenue
* Government adoption
* Legal and investigative adoption
* AI-ready data workflows
* Strong ecosystem of partners and contributors

## Long-Term Vision

FreeEed becomes the trusted platform that helps organizations understand, govern, review, disclose, and act on information.

From eDiscovery to investigations to AI readiness, FreeEed enables users to turn documents into knowledge while maintaining transparency, affordability, and human oversight.
